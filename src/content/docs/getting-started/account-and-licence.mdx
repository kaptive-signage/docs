# About your account

To get started with Kaptive, you'll need to create a free personal account and verify your email address.

You can also authenticate with Google or GitHub for a quicker sign-up process.

Once your account is set up, you will be asked to create a workspace where you can manage your projects and players. By default, you will have a free workspace with access to basic features.


# Signing up for a new account

<Steps>


    1. Navigate to [https://app.kaptive.ch/](https://app.kaptive.ch/).
    2. Click Sign up.
    3. Alternatively, click on Sign in with Google or Sign in with GitHub to sign up using social login.
    4. Follow the prompts to create your account.

</Steps>

# Choosing a licence

Kaptive offers different licence plans to suit your needs, including a free plan with basic features and paid plans with advanced capabilities. To learn more about our licence options, please [contact us].
