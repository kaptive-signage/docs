## What is Kaptive?

Kaptive is an interactive digital signage solution that allows you to create and publish your content on any screen in seconds.

### Quick Setup

1. **Create an account** - Sign up on [app.kaptive.ch](https://app.kaptive.ch)
2. **Connect a player** - Add a Kaptive player or web player to your account
3. **Create content** - Use our intuitive editor to create your screens
4. **Publish** - Broadcast your content instantly to your screens
